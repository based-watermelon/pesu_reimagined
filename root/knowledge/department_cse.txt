The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at PES University (PESU) is one of the institution's most prominent and highly ranked wings, recognized for its strong industry connections and high placement rates. 

Core Programs Offered
The department provides a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral degrees: 
Undergraduate:
Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science and Engineering.
B.Tech in CSE with specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI & ML).
Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) with tracks in System Administration and Application Design.
Postgraduate:
Master of Computer Applications (MCA) with specializations like Development Stack and Data Analytics.
M.Tech and M.Tech by Research in CSE.
Doctoral: Ph.D. programs for research aspirants.

Key Highlights
Placements: The department is famous for its placement outcomes. The average package for CSE students typically ranges between ₹10–12 LPA, with top offers exceeding ₹50–65 LPA from recruiters like Amazon, Microsoft, and Cisco.
Research & Innovation: It houses specialized labs such as the PESU-Huawei Innovation Lab and the Center for Information Security, Forensics and Cyber Security.
Rankings: PESU is consistently ranked among the top 100-150 engineering institutions in India by NIRF.
Campuses: Programs are offered at both the Ring Road (RR) Campus (Main) and the Electronic City (EC) Campus.

Admission & Fees (2026)
Admission Routes: Selection is primarily through PESSAT (university's entrance exam), KCET, and JEE Main.
Fees: The total academic fee for the B.Tech CSE program is approximately ₹22.1 Lakhs for the four-year duration.
