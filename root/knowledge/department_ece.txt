The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) at PES University is a major academic unit focused on core technology and interdisciplinary research across both the Ring Road (RR) and Electronic City (EC) campuses. 
Programs and Specializations
The department offers a curriculum that balances theoretical knowledge with project-based learning. 
B.Tech in ECE: A 4-year undergraduate program covering VLSI design, embedded systems, signal processing, and IoT.
M.Tech: Specialized postgraduate programs, notably in VLSI Design and Embedded Systems.
Ph.D. and M.Tech by Research: Research-focused degrees for students aiming for academia or high-level R&D. 

Placements (2024–2025)
While Computer Science is the flagship, ECE students at PES benefit from a robust placement ecosystem that includes both core and IT companies. 
Salary Statistics: The highest package for ECE students has reached ₹55–65 LPA. The average package for top branches like ECE generally ranges between ₹10–12 LPA.
Recruiters: Top companies include Qualcomm, Texas Instruments, Intel, AMD, Bosch, Samsung R&D, and Cisco. Many ECE students also secure roles in IT giants like Microsoft and Amazon due to strong programming skills.
Internships: Over 1,250 internship offers are facilitated annually, many of which convert into Pre-Placement Offers (PPOs). 

Facilities and Research
Specialized Labs: The department hosts advanced labs such as the VLSI Design Lab, Signal Processing & System Engineering Lab, and the WiNC (Wireless Networks and Communication) center.
Student Chapters: Active participation is encouraged in technical bodies like the IEEE Student Branch, which is one of the largest in South India.
Faculty: The department is led by experienced chairpersons (Dr. Shikha Tripathi at RR Campus and Dr. Ajey S N R at EC Campus) with a high percentage of Ph.D. holders among the staff. 

Admissions & Capacity
Intake: The Electronic City campus has a specific intake of 180 seats for B.Tech ECE, including quotas for CET and JEE Main.
Cost: The total tuition fee for the B.Tech ECE program is approximately ₹18–22 Lakhs depending on the admission category.
