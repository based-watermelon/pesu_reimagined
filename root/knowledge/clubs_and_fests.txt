PES University (PESU) features a vibrant student life with numerous clubs covering technical, cultural, and literary interests, alongside major fests like the annual techno-cultural fest, Aatmatrisha. Popular clubs include AI WEEK (AURA), coding (Alcoding), gaming (GameTalk - Parallax), and performing arts (Sanskriti, Team Trance). 

Major Fests and Events
Aatmatrisha: The annual flagship techno-cultural fest of PES University.
Epsilon: Annual national intercollegiate science fest.
MYSTARA: A 24-hour innovation marathon/hackathon hosted by the INIT Club.
AI WEEK 2025: Organized by the AURA Club.
Constellations: National level inter-collegiate quiz fest.
Samvaada 4.0: Organized by the Faculty of Law.
Vivacity'23: A health-focused fest organized by the WEAL club. 

Key Clubs and Student Groups
Technical & Academic: IEEE PESU, The Alcoding Club, Quantum Computing Club (QForest), Team Gronit (GreenGlitch), InsightX (DecodeX).
Cultural & Performing Arts: Sanskriti (Dance), Team Trance (Western/Bollywood Dance), Abhinayaveethii (Drama), Ninaada (Cultural night).
Media & Literary: HoPES PESU (Videography), PIXELS (Photography), PESU Talkies (Filmmaking), Write Angle.
Management & Others: Equinox (The Briefing Room), MINERVA (Frames of Campus), IRA PESU.
