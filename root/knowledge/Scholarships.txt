PES University (PESU) offers significant scholarships based on academic merit, including the Prof. MRD Scholarship (50% tuition fee waiver for top 5% of students) and the Prof. CNR Rao Scholarship (40% tuition fee waiver for top 20% of students). Other awards include distinctions, corporate, and rural scholarships. 

PES University
 
Key PESU Scholarship Programs:
* Prof. MRD Scholarship: 50% tuition fee refund for the top 5% of students based on CGPA for all years except the final year.
* Prof. CNR Rao Scholarship: 40% tuition fee refund for the top 20% of students based on SGPA.
* Distinction Awards: Cash prize for students achieving a CGPA of 7.5 or higher for a given year.
* Rural Scholarship: Free engineering education for a select number of students from rural backgrounds.
* Corporate Scholarships: Offered in the final year of education, often recognized by companies for high merit.
* Research/Teaching Assistantships: Compensation for students assisting professors or conducting research.

Important Notes:
* Scholarships are typically based on performance in the previous semester (SGPA) or year (CGPA) and are not applicable to the final year of study.
* The student must maintain the required CGPA/SGPA set by the committee to retain the scholarship.
* Only one scholarship can typically be utilized at a time
